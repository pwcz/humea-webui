| 102   | Night light level     | str     | '0', '1', '2', '3'      ||
| 103   | Sleep mode            | bool    | True/False              ||
| 104   | Auto mode             | bool    | True/False              ||
| 105   | Humidity settings     | str     | '0', '1', ..., '8'      ||
| 106   | Moisture output level | integer | '40', '45', '50', '55'  ||
| 107   | Filter status (??)    | str     | '0', '1'                ||
| 108   | Timer settings        | str     | '0', '1', ..., '12'     | hours |
| 109   | Humidity              | integer | 0-100                   | %     |
